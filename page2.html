Meatloaf Balls

A twist on the traditional meatloaf. A cross between meatloaf and meatballs.
Ground beef, onion, ritz crackers, eggs, milk, salt, and pepper.

2 lbs. ground beef
1 onion, diced
1 sleeve of Ritz Crackers
2 eggs
1/4 cup milk, if it seems dry
Salt and pepper

Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Spray cooking spray into muffin tin. Smash crackers with your fingers and place in a large bowl.
Add other ingredients, except for meat and milk. Use fork to mix together.
Add meat and work through with your fingers. You may add milk if it seems too dry. (I never have)
Form meatloaf mixture into balls about the right size for the muffin tin.
Cook muffin tin over a cookie sheet to catch grease that overflows. Cook for about 30 minutes or until it reaches 165.
Use thermometer to ensure the center of the center meatloaf is 165 degrees.
To add more flavor you could add ketchup
